COVID-19 Global Epidemic

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

COVID-19 Outbreak Map

This map shows the confirmed cases per million in the different country.The United States of America is the most affected country. Although China is the first country that discovered the COVID-19, the number of total cases per million is the lowest.

Confirmed cases and Deaths per 300 population

In the world, for every 300 people, approximately 9 people will be diagnosed with COVID-19, and 2 people will die due to being infected by the COVID-19.

Boxplot of total cases per 10,000

This box plot shows the outliers and distribution of confirmed cases per 10,000 in different continents. Africa has the most outliers, indicating that the confirmed cases per 10,000 among African countries are very different.

Deaths of COVID-19

The mutated COVID-19 virus was discovered in the UK in September 2020, which resulted in the death cases of Europe increased significantly from September 2020 to January 2021.

COVID-19 Vaccination Situation

At present, 47% of people in the world have been vaccinated. Asia has the largest number of vaccinated people in the world, while South America has the highest vaccination rate out of all continent. From the country's perspective, the United Arab Emirates is the highest vaccination rate country.

Correlation between death cases and confirmed cases

This bubble plot shows a positive correlation between the number of deaths and the number of confirmed cases. However, the population density does not show a positive correlation with the number of confirmed cases as expected. Many countries have low population density but have high total cases per million.
