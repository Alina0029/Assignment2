How has the market changed over time?

The western side of Melbourne has not changed much over time however the eastern and southern parts of Melbourne seem to generally increase suggesting they are much more popular areas to invest in.

Does dwelling type matter?

Understandably, houses are much more costly in comparison to townhouses and units as they are much smaller in land size. There are significantly more units in Southern and Northern Metropolitan, this could either be due to these areas being considerably close to the CBD or the popularity in housing in these areas. Eastern Metropolitan has fewer number of dwellings in comparison to Northern, Western and Southern. This could potentially be caused by the land size being larger in the east.
